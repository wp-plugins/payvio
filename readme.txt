== Description ==

wordpress paywall solutions for digital media publishers

Payvio let's you sell your way. Offer yearly, monthly, weekly or daily subscriptions. Sell digital media on an a la carte basis for a one time fixed fee. Sell your content for prices as low as a penny. Payvio is the most flexible payment solution available.

== Installation ==

After you have installed the plugin through your wordpress admin portal, simply fill out the plugin settings with information obtained by logging in to your payvio seller account.
